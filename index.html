Anime Zone

Kessoku Band in "Bocchi the Rock!" is a high school band comprised of four girls: Bocchi the guitarist, who overcomes shyness through her music; Nijika the drummer, known for her boundless energy and motivation; Ryo the bassist, who brings a cool and laid-back vibe with her groovy basslines; and Ikuyo the vocalist, distinguished by her vibrant personality and colorful fashion sense. Together, they navigate personal challenges, grow as musicians, and forge strong friendships filled with humor and heart.
